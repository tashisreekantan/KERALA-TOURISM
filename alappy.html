ALAPPUZHA

Situating in the Town Alappuzha district of Kerala Alappuzha or previously and more popularly known as Alleppey is one of the most important tourist destination of Godâ€™s Own Country - Kerala. Enamoured by the enchanting scenic beauty of this backwater queen, Lord Curzon, the then viceroy of India (1st Decade of 20th Century) expressed his felicity by calling Alleppey the Venice of the East. The city is still famous with this sobriquet. There are numerous canals, beaches and especially backwaters which have made this place the most attractive tourist spot in Kerala. Alappuzha is also the headquarters of Alappuzha district.
